Training and Workshops:

Mental health is just as important as physical health, especially in the fast-paced and often stressful environment of university life. Our platform is designed to support students and teachers by raising awareness about mental well-being and providing practical tools to cope with everyday challenges. Every month, we host free workshops and training sessions on topics such as stress management, emotional resilience, mindfulness, and communication skills. These sessions are open to all and led by professionals who understand the unique struggles faced by the academic community. Through this initiative, we aim to break the stigma around mental health and build a more compassionate, supportive campus environment where everyone feels safe, heard, and empowered. For more information or to inquire about our training programs, please contact us at info@soulsupport.org Join us in making a difference through the power of befriending.
