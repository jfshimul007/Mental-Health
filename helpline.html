"Helpline Service"

Soul Support is the first and only emotional support and suicide prevention helpline in Premier University, staffed by trained volunteers, where student and teacher can call to receive immediate emergency support. The mission of the helpline is to alleviate feelings of despair, isolation, distress, and suicidal intent among members of our community. Soul Support accomplishes this through confidential, compassionate, and open-minded listening.
